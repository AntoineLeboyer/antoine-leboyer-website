About

A career at the intersection of tech, venture & culture

Antoine Leboyer is the Managing Director of the TUM Venture Lab for Software and Artificial Intelligence, where he advises over a hundred university-led startups.

From 2008 to 2020 he served as President and CEO of the Swiss software group GSX, leading it through international expansion until its acquisition by Martello Technology. His entrepreneurial journey became the subject of a Harvard Business School case study — taught in MBA programmes worldwide.

He initiated Virtual Hravuta — a Torah-focused AI chatbot — and a member of the boards of both tech companies and cultural organisations. A classical music journalist at heart, he brings rare intellectual breadth to every stage and boardroom.
